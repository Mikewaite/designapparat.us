THE BEST KIND OF WORK IS COMPLEX

The most common thing I hear as a designer is "Can you just make this look good?". I often hear this question when a business or website is really simple in concept. When projects are complex, covering multiple devices or doing multiple things, you never really hear that sort of question. Instead, making a complicated project look good takes a lot of planning, structure and restraint. I like making things look good, but I enjoy making applications that help people accomplish their goals effortlessly even more.
