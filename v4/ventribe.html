Chipping in to buy grandma an iPad

VenTribe connects friends, families and communities through group gift buying. Members of the site can easily choose a gift, create a gift page and invite friends via private Facebook messages to chip in together for it. There is no cash transactions on the site itself, instead each member of a group gift pays independently for a gift, making this the ultimate way for social web to expand in the vast Facebook network.

VenTribe was in the early throes of a small business. They are made up of a bunch of MBA backgrounds who saw an opportunity to make money via Facebook. As a small company new to the ways of application development, they were already burned by a bunch of small design and development shops happy to take their money without adhering to an overall quality that they wanted. I worked as a design consultant with them to structure their site and business in way that can be communicated to 3rd party developers that would lead to a quality that they were missing. We spent time looking at comparable businesses and hacked our variations via paper sketches and white board sessions.
