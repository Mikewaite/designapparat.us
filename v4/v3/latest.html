How CBS released 1,000 apps at the same time

  • Client Strategy

  • UX Planning

  • UI Design

CBS Interactive created native mobile applications for over 100 universities. These schools each have over 20 sports to update and display information to their users. With the large number of sports and schools involved there are many individual app like experiences that are rolled into a single app. The challenge here was to both make this an app that would make the obscure sports fan happy in following 1 sport and the fan that wants to follow every sport for a school. On top of that, we also had to make this format work on iOS, Android and Blackberry. This increased the variations of the same app immensely.

I was the primary designer for this project. All of the planning, strategy and design work was done by me and subsequently a larger team after launch. CBS wanted to see this go live as soon as possible so I sketched out a bunch of concept ideas on a whiteboard with a team and dove right into the interaction design. Even today with all the individual stakeholders on a project like this, the apps are always changing and being modified for the latest sports season.
