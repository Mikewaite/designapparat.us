Using iPhones and iPads to monitor your workforce with information

  • UX Planning

  • UI Design

Work Crews is an application that works on both iPhones and iPads to share and update information with your field service team. Foremen clock crew members in and out on iPhones. Supervisors and owners schedule and monitor progress throughout the day on an iPad. They can also exchange text, photos, audio, and video with team members to keep everyone on task every day.

When I started on this project, the app was primarily iPhone focused. I was brought in to visualize how the concept of Work Crews could be used on a tablet like an iPad. I worked with client owners and the project stakeholders to sketch and mock up visual prototypes. Developers stayed in constant contact to tweak and modify design changes for a quick and easy beta release.
