Removing the paperwork from first responders so they can focus on saving lives

  • UX Planning

  • UI Design

ePCR is an iPad app that reduces the paperwork of patient care reports for medical first responders. The entire process of filling out paperwork, organizing that process and finally inputting that data has been streamlined into a single app allowing EMS and fire agencies to focus on their jobs. It makes electronic reporting affordable to organizations of all sizes and offers more accurate, complete and reliable reports to hospitals.

My involvement with this project included everything from concept to interface design. I started this project by walking into a meeting room with my client and on the table was about 20 pages of forms and procedures that needed to be digested and re-imagined from an electronic workflow perspective. The app has gone through a number of changes over time but it still delivers on a strong user experience workflow.
