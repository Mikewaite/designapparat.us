Bringing local news to over 100 apps

  • UX Planning

  • UI Design

Raycom Media group is the owner of some 30 or more local news stations throughout America. I helped these stations with designs and workflow for mobile applications across iPhone, iPad, Android and Blackberry devices. Mobile app work at this level is very complicated. Often I had to deal with very messy and complicated API data that had to be restructured from the ground up. Getting this data to work consistently across multiple devices was difficult because of the balance of performance and various user interfaces. The apps delivered local news, weather and sports in addition to unique data streams based on cultural interests. So yes, you can find the price of cattle somewhere on a local news app.
