Replacing paper menus with iPads

I suppose the idea of ordering your food with an iPad was an inevitability. Tiare was looking for an app to not only replace paper menus in Hyatt bars and restaurants, but also to be used as a point of sale device for patrons to order food and drinks. This is not a new idea per se, you can find lots of this sort of thing in more discreet high-end businesses like wineries and cruise ships. The challenge for Tiare was to customize a menu app for once business with multiple restaurants. Design restraints were based around understanding the most complicated ways of ordering food and drinks and creating an interface that works for any individual or a party of 10.
